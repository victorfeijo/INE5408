stp4020 <-> STP 4020 PCMCIA Adapter
The STP 4020 PCMCIA Adapter provides for two PCMCIA PC Card sockets. The stp4020 adapter driver pro<hy>
vides an interface between the PCMCIA sockets and the PCMCIA nexus. The driver supports the Sun PCMCIA
Interface/Sbus card.
Direct access to the PCMCIA hardware is not supported. The driver exists solely to support the PCMCIA nexus.
