pcmciad <-> PCMCIA user daemon
/usr/lib/pcmciad
The PCMCIA user daemon provides user-level services for the PCMCIA nexus driver and PCMCIA card client
drivers. There are no user-configurable options for this daemon.
See attributes(5) for descriptions of the following attributes:
