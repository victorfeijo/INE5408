ibmf <-> InfiniBand Management Transport Framework
The InfiniBand (IB) Management Transport Framework provides the mechanisms for IB management modules to
communicate with other InfiniBand management modules such as the Subnet Administration process. It also pro<hy>
vides helper functions such as Subnet Administration Access (SAA) for commonly performed operations.
